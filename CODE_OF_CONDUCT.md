# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

*وَتَعَاوَنُوا عَلَى الْبِرِّ وَالتَّقْوَىٰ*  
*"Cooperate in righteousness and piety."* — Quran 5:2

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in Ijma Wallet
a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless
of age, background, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity.

---

## Our standards

**Behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback graciously
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Showing empathy towards other contributors
- Acknowledging the contributions of others with credit

**Behaviour that is not acceptable:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Deliberate misgendering or use of preferred names/pronouns that a person
  has explicitly rejected
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information (doxing) without explicit permission
- Sexualised language, imagery, or unwelcome advances
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional open source community

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — GitHub issues, PRs,
discussions, the project Discord or Nostr channels, and any other official
communication channel. It also applies when an individual is representing the
project in public spaces.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be
reported to the project maintainer at:

**ijma@blockchainology.co.uk**

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The
maintainer is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.

**Enforcement actions**, in order of severity:

1. **Correction** — A private written warning with an explanation of the
   violation and why the behaviour was inappropriate.

2. **Warning** — A formal warning with consequences for continued behaviour.
   No interaction with the people involved for a specified period.

3. **Temporary ban** — A temporary ban from any interaction with the project
   for a specified period. Violation leads to a permanent ban.

4. **Permanent ban** — A permanent ban from any public interaction within
   the project community.

---

## Our values

This project is built on the principle that technology should serve humanity
with integrity. We welcome contributors of all faiths, backgrounds, and
convictions. The Islamic values embedded in Ijma — sovereignty, honesty,
justice, and the avoidance of harm — are project values, not exclusionary ones.
They are an invitation to build something better, together.
